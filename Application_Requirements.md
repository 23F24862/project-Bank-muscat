# Application Requirements (Bank Muscat Appraisal Platform)

## Overview

This application provides a platform for customers, appraisal companies, and bank administrators to manage property and vehicle appraisal workflows. It includes secure login, request tracking, report uploads, and admin oversight.

---

## 1. Customer (Client) Functions

- **Account Registration & Login** – Create and access a secure account.
- **Browse Appraisal Companies** – View approved companies with filters (location, type, rating).
- **View Company Profiles** – Check services, licenses, contact details, and ratings.
- **Submit Appraisal Request** – Upload documents for property/vehicle assessment.
- **Track Request Status** – Pending, under review, incomplete docs, in progress, completed.
- **Receive Appraisal Report** – Download final PDF report.
- **Rate the Company** – Provide rating & feedback.

---

## 2. Appraisal Company Functions

- **Secure Login** – Access company dashboard.
- **Receive Requests** – View customer requests and attached documents.
- **Review & Respond** – Accept/reject, request more documents, schedule inspections.
- **Perform Appraisal** – Conduct evaluation.
- **Upload Appraisal Report** – Upload final PDF and mark as completed.
- **Update Company Profile** – Manage licenses, contact info, services.
- **Ensure Compliance** – Follow bank policies.

---

## 3. Bank Administrator Functions

- **Admin Login** – Secure dashboard access.
- **Manage Appraisal Companies** – Add, update, suspend or remove companies.
- **Monitor All Requests** – Track all appraisal activity.
- **Ensure Compliance** – Verify legitimacy, legal standards, and quality.
- **Manage Users & Permissions** – Handle all accounts.
- **Generate Reports** – Performance, compliance, and usage statistics.
- **System Communication** – Send alerts and policy updates.

---

## 4. Full User & Admin Instructions

### Customer Steps

1. Sign in using email/phone.
2. Browse authorized companies.
3. Use filters to select company.
4. View license & service details.
5. Submit appraisal request + documents.
6. Track request status.
7. Download report when ready.
8. Rate the company.

### Appraisal Company Steps

1. Log in securely.
2. Review assigned requests.
3. Verify documents.
4. Accept and schedule inspection.
5. Conduct appraisal.
6. Upload final PDF report.
7. Mark request completed.
8. Maintain updated company profile.

### Bank Administrator Steps

1. Sign in securely.
2. Manage companies.
3. Validate licenses.
4. Monitor all activity.
5. Review reports.
6. Generate performance insights.
7. Send alerts/notifications.
8. Manage permissions and security.

---
